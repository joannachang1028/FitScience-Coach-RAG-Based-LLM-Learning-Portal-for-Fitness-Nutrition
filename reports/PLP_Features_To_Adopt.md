### Features to Adopt in FitScience Coach
1. Progress Dashboard with Goal Tracking
   - Daily/weekly progress on calories, workouts, steps/NEAT, sleep
   - Visuals: progress bars, goal vs. actual charts, streak counters

2. Interactive Q&A with Citations ("Ask Coach")
   - Retrieval-augmented answers with inline citations and source badges
   - Expandable reference drawer (title, URL, type, quick relevance note)

3. Personalized Plan Builder
   - Wizard for goals, schedule, equipment, preferences, constraints
   - Outputs: weekly training split + macro/calorie targets; editable

4. Module-Based Learning Paths
   - Short modules: Calories & BMR/TDEE; Macros; Micronutrients; Training Basics; Sleep & Recovery
   - Quizzes/reflections to reinforce learning and track understanding

5. Evidence & Logs Workspace
   - Saved answers, plan versions, evaluation logs (RAGAs/ARES), and notes
   - Export as CSV/Markdown for assignment deliverables

### Rationale
- Mirrors PLP best practices (modular content, personalization, progress)
- Emphasizes transparency with citations and logged evaluations
- Balances guidance (coach-like UX) with self-paced learning modules
